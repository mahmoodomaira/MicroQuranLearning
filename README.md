# 📖 Micro Quran Learning

A personal desktop app for reading, understanding, and memorizing the Quran — one verse at a time.

---

## What it does

Micro Quran Learning displays verses from **Juz Amma** (the 30th part of the Quran) with Arabic text and English translation side by side. It is designed to support three different use cases: casual reading, active memorization, and recitation during prayer.

---

## Features

### 📚 Reading Mode
Browse all verses of Juz Amma one ayah at a time. Navigate forward and backward freely, or jump directly to any surah using the dropdown menu.

### 🧠 Memorization Mode
Hide all Arabic words at once and reveal them one by one as you recall them. The app tracks your progress within the verse and resets automatically when you move to the next one.

### 🕌 Prayer Mode
A distraction-free view designed for recitation. All controls are hidden, the Arabic text enlarges, and you can advance to the next verse with the spacebar — no mouse needed.
